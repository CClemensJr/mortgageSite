GREAT ODIN'S RAVENS!

The raven has mythical powers and has saved Odin from a variety of jams!

  • The ravens defeated the triceratops of Farth while Odin was helpless and indisposed.
  • The ravens wake Odin up at 3am every morning so that he can maintain his cut physique.
  • They drove Odin home when he was too drunk to drive.
  • The ravens also taught baby Thor how to fetch hammers.
